ProvinceGuessr | 猜菜系戏

How well do you know the various dishes of China? This game will display various dishes from each province, and your task is to guess where the food originates from.

All provinces, administrative regions, and autonomous regions are included for a total of 34 distinct options.

Hover over a province to see its name. When you are ready to guess, click and submit. If you get lost, feel free to reset your view.
